# Day-0 Launch Plan

Date: 2026-02-18

## T-24h
- Finalize app screenshots and preview video.
- Verify legal URLs are live and HTTPS.
- Run: `./launch/prelaunch-audit.sh your-domain.com`.
- Confirm App Store / Play metadata copy-paste is ready.

## T-2h
- Publish/update landing page.
- Submit sitemap in GSC and Bing.
- Run IndexNow ping: `./website/indexnow-submit.sh your-domain.com`.
- Prepare social posts in draft mode.

## T-0
- Publish app listing(s).
- Publish launch post on main channel.
- Send email list announcement.
- Ask close friends/community for first installs + reviews.

## T+6h
- Reply to comments/messages.
- Collect first bug reports.
- Track top user questions and update support FAQ.

## T+24h
- Post a mini-update with first metrics.
- Prioritize top 3 UX fixes from feedback.
