# Community Outreach Pack

Date: 2026-02-18

## Goal
Get first 30-50 real users in the first week.

## Who to contact first
- Close friends using receipts/invoices often.
- Freelancers and small business owners.
- Student communities handling admin paperwork.

## Message template (DM)
"Je lance une app mobile qui scanne et classe les documents 100% hors ligne.
Tu peux tester et me dire ce qui bloque en 2 minutes ?
Lien: https://scannerdocument.vercel.app/"

## 3 asks to maximize support
1. Install and run one real scan.
2. Report one UX pain point.
3. Leave a store rating if useful.

## Follow-up after 48h
- Thank users personally.
- Share fixes released from their feedback.
- Ask for referral to 1 more person.
